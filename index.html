Our Story

A Café Born From Passion

Brûlée started as a small corner shop with one espresso machine and a dream. Today, we're a beloved neighbourhood staple — but the heart hasn't changed. Every cup is pulled with intention. Every dish is cooked from scratch.

We source our beans from single-origin farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra. Our kitchen changes its menu with the seasons, honouring what the earth gives us.

🫘 Single Origin Traceable beans, roasted in-house weekly
🌱 Sustainable Zero-waste kitchen, compostable packaging
👩‍🍳 Made Fresh Every item prepared to order, daily
